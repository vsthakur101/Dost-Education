Supporting vulnerable families with credible information and learning resources


The COVID-19 pandemic has created stress and panic worldwide, and the lack of access to reliable information only compounds the problem. It is especially hard for low-income, low-literacy families who are concerned about their livelihood, health and safety. We are helping the most vulnerable families who have limited access to internet, credible information, and empathetic support by offering audio resources they can access from their homes


Dost's audio program

Families who join the service will receive daily 1-minute phone calls with credible information and resources.(currently available within India and in Hindi - contact us for resources in other languages)

Covid

2  Weeks

  • Addressing stress, positive behavior management for families, and hygiene techniques to adopt at home (sourced from WHO)
Learning At Home

24  Weeks

  • Parenting strategies and learning activities for kids aged 3-6 to help parents facilitate continued learning at home while schools are closed


A global problem


All parents are motivated to give their children the best education,but many resource constrained families struggle to do so, particularly in the early years.Experts agree on a cost-effective yet overlooked approach to address this cycle of illiteracy: empower parents to promote early childhood development at home.


An untapped opportunity


Dost Education empowers parents of any literacy level to take charge of their child’s early education.

A PROVEN MECHANISM

Evidence-based, parent approved

  • Getting kids school ready is not rocket science! We take the proven science behind early childhood development and turn it into actionable steps for parents of ANY literacy level to embed into daily routines.
A SCALABLE APPROACH

A phone call from a friend

  • We create short, friendly audio content that reaches parents where they already are: their mobile phones. Dost is cost-effective at scale without compromising on the ability to personalize content.
